Here comes the tofu

You will need:

  • 400 grams of tofu
  • 400 grams soba noodles
  • 2 oranges
  • a cup of vegetable broth or miso broth
  • 4 tbs of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbs of agave syrup
  • 3 tbs of soy sauce (light)
  • 2-3 tbs of gochugaru red chilli flakes
  • some minced ginger
  • some cornstarch
  • 5 cloves of garlic, minced
  • delicate oil
Garnish:
  • Some green onion
  • Roasted sesame seeds

The process:

  1. Start by cutting the tofu into cubes of preffered size, set aside.
  2. In a bowl combine: vegetable broth, juice from the two oranges, vinegar, agave syrup, soy sauce, chilli flakes, ginger and garlic. Stir until they combine.
  3. Marinate the tofu in the abovementioned mixture for some time (the longer the better preferably overnight).
  4. Remove the tofu from the marinade. Heat up a frying pan with a generous splash of oil. Fry the tofu until golden stiring whenever necessary.
  5. Once the tofu is ready, pour in the marinade and simmer under the lid for about 10 minutes.
  6. In the meantime: cook the noodles according to the instructions on the package. When they are ready - strain them and submerge in some cold water. Strain again and set aside.
  7. Reduce heat to mnimum and add some cornstarch to thicken the sauce (start with 1 tbs, add more if necessary). Stir frequently and after a while turn off the heat completely.
  8. Plate. Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds. The tofu itself tastes better the next day. Enjoy :)
